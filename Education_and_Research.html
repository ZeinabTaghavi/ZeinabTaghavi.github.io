Text Area Detection in Printed Documents with Transfer Learning

As part of my Bachelor's project and in association with Tooba Tech, I developed an approach for detecting text areas in printed documents using transfer learning. This project was part of the larger Resolution Enhancement project at Tooba Tech.

Resolution Enhancement

In my Bachelor's project and in association with Tooba Tech, I worked on improving the resolution of historical manuscripts using advanced computer vision and machine learning techniques.

Handwriting Manuscript Line and Segment Detection and Storage (Persian and Arabic Manuscript)

As part of my Bachelor's project and in association with Tooba Tech, I developed a system to detect and store lines and segments in handwritten Persian and Arabic manuscripts. This enabled easier access and analysis of historical documents.
